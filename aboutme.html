My Story

Born and raised in the fruitful parish of St. Elizabeth Jamaica. Shane's story all started at the age of 11 when he got his first desktop computer in the year 2006. He was so fascinated he began to wonder how the computer actually worked and what components were inside. He would take computers apart and to find the purpose of each component. He then went on to building and repairing computers. After he familiarized himself of how the components worked he then began to wonder what made all these flashy programs and computer games tick and that's what created the competent software developer before you today.
